Welcome to Emmanuel's Final Project page! & Thanks for visiting
I created this game becuase over the semester, learning how to program I was really interested in making games and designing them.
I was also inspired by my brother who is a computer engineer! to take on a challenge such as this, and I'm proud to say that I did it!
My program is a 2D Runner Game in which the player can be the main character, whhich is a styling of my emoji.
As the main character the objective is to score 100 points, 3 points are earned every time the user jumps over and lands over an obstacle.
If the user collides with the obstacle, 1 point is subtracted.
To jump over obstacles, the user must press or hold the space bar
To pause and or play the game while the game is already started, press the play/pause button at the top of the screen
The game continues until you win, after you have won press restart to play again
You must overcome all 100 obstacles!
Thank you and enjoy playing my fun and addicting game :)
